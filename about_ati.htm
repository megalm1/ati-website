Company Profile

 

Founded in 2005, ATI – Advanced Medical Technologies Ltd is pioneering the development of safe non-invasive diagnostic devices for the ophthalmology market in order to allow profesionals to give faster and safer treatment.

ATI has two novel products in its portfolio, a non-invasive pupil dilation device, called the FIM and a personal, mobile non-invasive tonometer to measure intraocular pressure, called the NIT.

 

ATI is a member of ILSI - Israel Life Science Industry.
