Products

ATI has two novel products in its portfolio, a non-invasive pupil dilation device, called the FIM and a personal, mobile non-invasive tonometer to measure intraocular pressure, called the NIT.

 

 FIM (Field Induced Mydriasis)

 

 

 NIT (Non-Invasive Tonometer)

  

 

  

 

The FIM is a novel non-invasive medical device that helps doctors to get pupil enlargement (Mydriasis) without the need of dropping eye drops substances (Mydramide Atropine, etc) into the patients eye. ATI already launched its first generation of FIM Prototype Devices and is actually starting it's Medical Research in one of the most qualified and  highly respected Medical Care Centers in Israel.

 

 

The NIT is a novel non-invasive device that helps Glaucoma tended or sick people and/or anyone above the age of forty years to measure their intra-ocular eye pressure (IOP) at any time day or night, in the privacy of their home, office or car. There is no need for professional assistance or time and money waste.  no need to set appointments with your eye M.D. in order to measure your IOP. You can meassure your IOP without the need of costly machinery or medical invasive substances.
